Professional Floor Care Services

Why Floor Care Matters

Floors are one of the most heavily trafficked parts of any home or business, and regular care is essential to maintain their beauty, safety, and longevity. Proper floor care prevents damage from dirt, spills, scratches, and wear, saving you money on costly repairs or replacements.

Our Comprehensive Floor Care Solutions

At PCD MoveInReady Inc., we provide specialized floor care services tailored to the unique needs of each flooring type. Our experienced technicians use industry-leading techniques and eco-friendly products to clean, restore, and protect your floors, including:

  • Hardwood floors
  • Tile and grout cleaning
  • Vinyl and linoleum care
  • Carpet cleaning and stain removal
  • Stone floor polishing and sealing

Step-by-Step Floor Care Process

Our floor care specialists follow a thorough process:

  • Initial inspection and preparation including furniture moving and surface clearing
  • Removal of dust, dirt, and debris by sweeping and vacuuming
  • Application of appropriate cleaning agents and scrubbing techniques
  • Polishing or sealing to restore shine and protect against future wear
  • Final quality check and customer walkthrough to ensure satisfaction

  • Improved indoor air quality by removing allergens and dust
